Instant, Secure, and Professional HR Documentation

Employees often need employment certificates for bank loans, visa applications, or personal records. SGC Employment Certificate automates this request-to-delivery cycle. Using QR code verification and digital signatures, you provide your employees with high-fidelity, branded certificates that can be instantly verified by third parties through a secure portal.
