# Deals Management Clean — User Guide

## Overview

Deals Management Clean provides end-to-end tracking of real estate deals and
commission workflows in Odoo 17. This guide focuses on day-to-day usage.

## Navigation

- **Sales → Deals**: All deal tracking views.
- **Sales → Commissions**: Commission lines, reports, and dashboards.
- **Sales → Deals → Projects/Units**: Project and unit tracking.

## Creating a Deal

1. Go to **Sales → Deals → All Deals**.
2. Click **Create**.
3. Fill in the required fields:
   - Sales Type
   - Primary Buyer
   - Project and Unit (if applicable)
   - Booking Date and Estimated Invoice Date
4. Upload documents in the **Deals Information** tab.
5. Save the record.

## Working with Documents

Use the smart buttons to view:

- KYC Documents
- Booking Forms / SPA
- Passports

## Commission Workflow (Typical)

1. Confirm the sale order and post at least one invoice.
2. Click **Process Commissions** on the sale order.
3. Review commission lines under **Sales → Commissions**.
4. Create vendor bills or purchase orders as needed.
5. Use the payment status and reports to track completion.

## Reports

Available reports include:

- Commission Payout Report
- Commission Partner Statement
- Team Commission Report

Reports are accessible from the **Sales → Commissions** menu or from the
related sale order smart buttons.

## Tips

- Use the **Commission by Partner** menu to group commissions by agent.
- Use filters for **Pending Bills** and **Paid Bills** for quick follow-up.
- Enable the **Commission Manager** group for full access.
