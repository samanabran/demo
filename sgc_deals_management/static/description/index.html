Unified Control for Complex Real Estate Transactions

Real estate operations often suffer from fragmented data across sales, commissions, and inventory. Deals Management Clean merges these critical functions into a single, production-ready Odoo suite. Manage your units, track buyers, handle multi-layered commission structures, and maintain a complete document repository in one seamless workflow.

Key Features

🏠

Deal Lifecycle Tracking

Comprehensive management for Off-Plan, Resale, and Rental deals with custom states and integrated document attachments.

🏗️

Project & Unit Inventory

Custom models for real estate projects and units, providing granular control over your available property stock and specifications.

💸

Advanced Commission Engine

Automated commission calculation, payout tracking, and full integration with Odoo's purchase and vendor bill workflows.

📂

Digital Document Vault

Securely store and manage KYC documents, booking forms, and passports directly within the deal record.

🧾

Financial Summaries

Automatic calculation of sales values, VAT, and totals, ensuring full financial transparency for every transaction.

📑

Professional Reporting

A suite of professional reports including commission payout reports and partner statements for external stakeholders.
