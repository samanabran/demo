SGC Assessment System

Next-generation AI-Powered Candidate Assessment and Evaluation System with NLP scoring and human review workflow.

Explore Features Get Started

Revolutionize Talent Discovery with AI Intelligence

Traditional screening processes are slow and often miss high-potential candidates. SGC Assessment System leverages NLP-driven AI scoring to analyze candidate responses across five critical dimensions: Technical, Sales, Communication, Learning, and Cultural Fit. By automating initial evaluations, your recruitment team can focus their energy on interviewing only the top-ranked talent.

Key Features

🧠

AI-Powered NLP Scoring

Sophisticated analysis of candidate responses to provide objective scores across multiple expertise categories.

🌐

Public Assessment Portal

Dedicated candidate-facing interface for seamless assessment submission, accessible via unique secure tokens.

👥

Human Review Workflow

Structured queue for recruiters and managers to review AI findings, add comments, and finalize rankings.

🏆

Candidate Rankings

Dynamic leaderboard that automatically ranks candidates based on their composite assessment scores.

Advanced Analytics

Comprehensive recruitment dashboards visualizing assessment funnels, scoring distributions, and candidate quality.
