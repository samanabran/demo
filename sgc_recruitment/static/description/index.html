Hiring in the UAE Made Simple & Compliant

Recruiting in the United Arab Emirates requires specialized compliance—from Emirates ID tracking to UAE Labour Law aligned benefits. SGC Recruitment extends standard Odoo functionality with UAE-specific localization. Automate your complex salary breakdowns, generate professional offer letters with digital signatures, and track visa sponsorships all within a single unified recruitment pipeline.
