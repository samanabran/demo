Closing the books shouldn't mean juggling nine different wizards and exports. Dynamic Financial Reports gives your finance team a single, configurable reporting engine covering every core statement — with multi-company support, flexible date ranges, period-over-period comparison, and account/analytic/partner filtering built in from the start.
